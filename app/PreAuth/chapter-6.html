CHAPTER 6

Proofing & Polish.

The final stretch. Before you hit submit, we need to ensure every pixel is aligned and every comma is in its place. Precision is the silent signal of professionalism.

fact_check

Mochii Checklist

  • The Spelling Sweep

    Check for "your/you're" and common typos that auto-correct misses.

  • Contact Info Verification

    Dial your own number. Click your own email link. Do they work?

  • Layout Alignment

    Check for "widows" (single words on a new line) and consistent margins.

spellcheck

The 10-Minute Rule

Step away from your screen for 10 minutes before the final proofread. Fresh eyes find the hidden flaws.
