Meet Mochii

Mochii wasn't always a tech-savvy cat. Legend has it, he spent months trying to format a two-column resume in a standard word processor, only for it to break every time he added a new skill.

After 42 rejection emails, all formatted beautifully by the companies themselves, Mochii decided that enough was enough. He teamed up with a human student to build a tool that handles the "scary tech" so you can focus on the words.
