Hey there, resume creator!

MeowFolio is local-first in this build. Your session, profile, resume drafts, and analysis results stay in your browser so you can keep working without sending your resume content to a backend service.

savings

Mochii's Pinky Promise

We don't sell your data. Not to advertisers, not to shadow corporations, not even for extra cat treats. Your info stays yours.

data_object What we collect

To build your resume, MeowFolio stores the basics you enter: name, contact info, summary, education, experience, projects, skills, template choices, and customization preferences. That data lives in local browser storage on the device you're using.

auto_fix_high How we use it

We use your data to populate the editor, render previews, calculate local ATS and JD scores, and let you export with your browser's print-to-PDF flow. In this static version, MeowFolio does not email your resume or upload it to a server.

auto_delete

Clean Paws Policy

Your original raw files are deleted from our temporary processing servers as soon as the parsing is finished. We only keep the structured data you see in your editor.

cookie Cookies & Biscuits

We primarily rely on local browser storage rather than tracking cookies. That local data keeps your session, active resume, and editor state available after refresh.

hub Third-party friends

This static build does not send your resume data to external processing services. The main third-party assets on these pages are presentation helpers like hosted fonts and the Tailwind CDN.
