01 ATS Compatibility

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) can struggle with the hidden XML markers in Microsoft Word documents. LaTeX produces "clean" PDFs where text is stored as pure data, ensuring recruiters' software parses every single one of your skills correctly.

PRO TIP

Standard Word columns often break reading order for bots. LaTeX handles layout through logical blocks, keeping your story linear for AI.
