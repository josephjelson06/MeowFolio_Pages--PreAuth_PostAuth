A Bullet Point's Soul

Recruiters spend 6 seconds per resume. They aren't looking for a list of chores. They are looking for Evidence of Value. Mochii learned that every bullet needs to be a mini-story with a beginning (the action), a middle (the task), and an end (the victory).
