Trust is Key!

1. Acceptance of Terms

By signing up or using MeowFolio, you're agreeing to these rules. It's a simple contract between you and the product, so please read it carefully.

If you don't agree, that's okay, but you won't be able to use the resume builder in this browser session.

2. Use of Service

You must be at least 13 years old to use MeowFolio. This version stores your session and drafts locally in your browser, so you are responsible for the device and browser profile where that data lives.

  • Don't try to hack our servers.
  • Don't scrape our data for your own AI.
  • Use your real name; nobody hires "X_DarkShadow_X".

3. Analysis Hints & Limits

Resume scoring, JD matching, and rewrite hints in this build are generated locally with deterministic heuristics. They are meant to guide revision, not replace your own review.

Suggestions may be incomplete or imperfect, so always double-check the final wording before exporting or applying.

4. Browser Export Limitations

Exports in this version rely on your browser's print and PDF capabilities. Layouts are designed to stay stable, but device settings, print margins, and browser quirks can still affect the final file.

Preview your resume before sharing it, especially after major content or template changes.

5. User Content

You own your data. Your resume content, profile fields, and analysis results remain yours.

In this local-first build, your information is stored in your browser unless you choose to export or manually share it elsewhere.

6. Liability

We provide this service "as is." We don't guarantee you'll get a job at Google. We are not responsible for any missed opportunities or typos that the AI might have suggested.

Double-check your phone number! We once had a user list "911" as their primary contact. Don't be that person.
