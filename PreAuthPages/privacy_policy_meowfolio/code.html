We know legal documents are usually a snooze-fest, but at DreamResume, we want to be 100% honest and open with you. Think of this as a pinky-promise between us and your career dreams. We treat your data with the same love you put into your CV.

Mochii's Pinky Promise

We don't sell your data. Not to advertisers, not to shadow corporations, not even for extra cat treats. Your info stays yours.

data_object What we collect

To build your dream resume, we need the basics: your name, contact info, and work history. If you're using our LaTeX export, we handle those curly braces and backslashes for you. We also keep a tiny record of your account settings so we remember your favorite templates.

auto_fix_high How we use it

Our smart parser (powered by some very clever code and a bit of Mochii's magic) reads your uploaded files to pre-fill your templates. We use your email to send you your polished PDFs and maybe a celebratory high-five when you finish your draft.

auto_delete

Clean Paws Policy

Your original raw files are deleted from our temporary processing servers as soon as the parsing is finished. We only keep the structured data you see in your editor.

cookie Cookies & Biscuits

We use "essential cookies" to keep you logged in. They’re like little digital bookmarks. We don't use creepy tracking cookies that follow you around the internet. That's just weird.

hub Third-party friends

We use a few trusted partners to keep things running—like our cloud hosting and secure payment processors (if you go Premium). They’re all vetted by Mochii for being trustworthy and keeping their digital yards clean.
